F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Will CogniAI hallucinate?

Our AI is designed to say "I don’t know" when uncertain, reducing the risk of unverified information. All responses are reviewed by humans for accountability and accuracy. With a focus on 95% precision and a 2.7% hallucination rate, CogniAI ensures reliable, accurate results.

Will my data be safe?

Rest assured, CogniAI will never use your data to train its models. We are deeply committed to protecting your data and adhere to stringent data privacy and security standards. Your data will not be used to train our LLMs or enhance other customers’ answers.

How quickly can we roll out a solution with CogniAI?

DocketAI collects all your structured and unstructured sources of knowledge in a few hours.

How do you govern platform access?

We have a robust admin platform that provides granular control over access rights, ensuring only authorized users can access sensitive data and features. Our system incorporates strong authentication mechanisms, to enhance security and ensure that access is granted based on roles and permissions.
